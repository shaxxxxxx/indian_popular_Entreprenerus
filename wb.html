Sundar Pichai

Early Life Born in Chennai in 1972, Sundar Pichai grew up in a modest two-room apartment. His father was an electrical engineer, and Pichai was introduced to technology through family discussions and his first rotary phone. Education Journey He studied Metallurgical Engineering at IIT Kharagpur, where he was awarded a silver medal. He later earned a Master’s in Material Sciences from Stanford and an MBA from Wharton, where he was a Siebel Scholar. Business Journey He joined Google in 2004 as a product manager and soon became instrumental in the development of Google Toolbar and Chrome, which changed the way people accessed the web. Over the years, he climbed ranks, managing Android, Chrome OS, and Google Apps. In 2015, he became CEO of Google, and in 2019, CEO of Alphabet Inc. Pichai is known for his humility, clarity, and user-first vision. Under his leadership, Google has grown into a global AI, cloud, and hardware powerhouse.
