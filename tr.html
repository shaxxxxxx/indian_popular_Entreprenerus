Satyabrata Deb

Early Life Born in Tripura, Satyabrata Deb showed early interest in life sciences and rural development. Despite facing infrastructural challenges, he pursued his education with dedication. Education Journey He earned a Ph.D. in Biotechnology and worked on agricultural genomics and organic farming systems. His education included stints in premier Indian labs and international research institutions. Business Journey Deb became a scientist-turned-entrepreneur, co-founding AgriGenomics Labs, which develops biofertilizers and sustainable farming solutions. His startup empowers farmers with data-driven tools and organic inputs. He also mentors agri-tech startups in Northeast India, helping bridge the gap between lab research and rural impact.
