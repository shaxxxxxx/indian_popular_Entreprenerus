Rahul Jain

Early Life Early Life Rahul Jain was born into a middle-class family in India and showed a deep curiosity for systems, business processes, and behavioral economics from a young age. He was analytical and had a knack for simplifying complex ideas, which later shaped his approach to entrepreneurship and coaching. Education Journey He earned his engineering degree followed by an MBA in International Business. His education focused not only on academic excellence but also on understanding the real-world challenges of SMEs and startups. Business Journey Rahul is the founder of Bizwiz, a platform dedicated to helping small and medium-sized businesses scale using structured systems and processes. He’s one of the pioneers in business coaching in India and has trained over 25,000 entrepreneurs. Through his signature programs and workshops, he emphasizes the power of automation, delegation, and mindset transformation. Rahul advocates for system-driven businesses instead of people-dependent ones, empowering founders to achieve growth without burnout..
