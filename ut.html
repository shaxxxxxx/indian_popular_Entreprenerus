Sanjay Agarwal

Early Life Sanjay was born in Jaipur, Rajasthan. From early on, he showed an interest in finance and economics, particularly in empowering small businesses. Education Journey He is a Chartered Accountant, ranked among the top scorers in his batch. His education gave him deep insights into financial systems, lending, and compliance. Business Journey He founded AU Financiers in 1996 to provide vehicle loans to underserved populations. Over two decades, the company transformed into AU Small Finance Bank, one of India’s most successful transitions from an NBFC to a bank. Sanjay focused on financial inclusion, building trust among rural and semi-urban customers. Today, AU Bank serves millions with digital banking, loans, and insurance, and is publicly listed with strong performance.
