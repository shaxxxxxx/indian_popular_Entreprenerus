Sanjay Giri

Early Life Sanjay Giri grew up in Odisha, India, where he developed a strong connection to his roots and regional culture. His early interest was in art, storytelling, and tribal heritage, which shaped his later initiatives. Education Journey He pursued a degree in Mass Communication and Design, followed by studies in marketing and branding. His education combined creative thinking with business acumen. Business Journey Sanjay founded Odishay, a platform that promotes Odisha's tribal crafts, textiles, and art through e-commerce and exhibitions. He has created a bridge between traditional artisans and modern markets. Through community engagement and sustainable practices, Odishay empowers tribal artisans, especially women, to become economically independent. His work has also received recognition for preserving intangible cultural heritage while promoting entrepreneurship.
